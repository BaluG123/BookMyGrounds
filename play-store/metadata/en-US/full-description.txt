BookMyGrounds helps players discover quality sports venues and reserve slots without the usual back-and-forth.

Players can browse grounds, compare timings, pick slots, apply eligible promo or referral codes, and complete payments in one flow.

Ground owners get a clean operating console for managing venues, tracking revenue and conversion, handling booking requests, updating slots, and keeping payout details ready for settlement.

Key features:
- Live ground discovery across popular sports
- Slot selection with pricing visibility
- Secure payment flow with Razorpay
- Booking history, status tracking, and reviews
- Owner dashboard with revenue and conversion insights
- Promo and referral code support
- Notifications for booking and payment events
