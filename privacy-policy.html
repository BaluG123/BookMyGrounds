🏏 Privacy Policy

BookMyGrounds - Sports Ground Booking Platform

Last Updated: April 22, 2026

Welcome to BookMyGrounds! We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and services.

📱 App Information:
App Name: BookMyGrounds
Package Name: in.bookmygrounds.app
Website: www.bookmygrounds.in

1Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

When you register and use BookMyGrounds, we may collect the following personal information:

  • Account Information: Full name, email address, phone number, profile picture
  • Authentication Data: Google Sign-In credentials (email, name, profile photo)
  • Booking Information: Booking history, payment details, preferences
  • Location Data: GPS coordinates to show nearby grounds and provide navigation
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers

1.2 Ground Owner Information

If you register as a ground owner, we additionally collect:

  • Ground details (name, address, photos, amenities, pricing)
  • Business information and verification documents
  • Bank account details for payment processing
  • Booking and revenue analytics

1.3 Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: App interactions, features used, time spent
  • Log Data: IP address, browser type, access times
  • Cookies: Session cookies for authentication and preferences

2How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

2.1 Service Delivery

  • Process and manage bookings
  • Facilitate payments through Razorpay
  • Provide customer support
  • Send booking confirmations and reminders
  • Show nearby sports grounds based on your location

2.2 Communication

  • Send transactional emails and SMS notifications
  • Provide updates about your bookings
  • Send promotional offers and discounts (with your consent)
  • Respond to your inquiries and support requests

2.3 Improvement and Analytics

  • Analyze app usage to improve features
  • Monitor and prevent fraud
  • Conduct research and analytics
  • Personalize your experience

3Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

3.1 With Ground Owners

When you make a booking, we share your name and phone number with the ground owner to facilitate your booking.

3.2 Service Providers

We share information with trusted third-party service providers:

  • Firebase: Authentication, push notifications, analytics
  • Google Sign-In: Authentication services
  • Razorpay: Payment processing
  • Google Maps: Location services and navigation
  • PythonAnywhere: Backend hosting

3.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or government request, or to protect our rights and safety.

3.4 Business Transfers

In case of merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred to the new entity.

4Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: All data transmission is encrypted using SSL/TLS
  • Secure Storage: Data is stored on secure servers with access controls
  • Authentication: Token-based authentication for API requests
  • Payment Security: We do not store credit card information; payments are processed securely through Razorpay
  • Regular Audits: We conduct regular security audits and updates
⚠️ Important: While we strive to protect your information, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

5Your Rights and Choices

5.1 Access and Update

You can access and update your personal information through the app's profile settings.

5.2 Delete Account

You can request account deletion by contacting us at support@bookmygrounds.in. We will delete your data within 30 days, except where required by law.

5.3 Opt-Out of Communications

You can opt-out of promotional emails and notifications through app settings or by clicking "unsubscribe" in emails.

5.4 Location Services

You can disable location access through your device settings, but this may limit app functionality.

5.5 Data Portability

You can request a copy of your data in a machine-readable format by contacting us.

7Children's Privacy

BookMyGrounds is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us, and we will delete such information.

8Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations:

  • Active Accounts: Data retained while account is active
